Tim Robotika Universitas Indonesia

Place for every UI student who wants to seek and develop their passion in Automations, Controls, Embedded Systems, and Robotics. TRUI facilitates every UI student from any major to participates and wins in any robotic competition like Kontes Robot Nasional (KRN) which held by Dirjen DIKTI.

TRUI is the leading organization in research and development of robotics which always uphold the value of friendship, works hard, and fun.

Divisions

TRUI has 4 functional divisions

Artificial Intelligence

implement algorithm on the robot so it able to meet any specific condition

Electrical

design and build the electrical component which are needed by the robot so it can adapt their environment

Mechanical

design and realize the physical form of the robot and make them to be able to move freely in any specific condition

Managerial

manage, organize, and maintain the performance of the team and lead everyone to move together keep on their feet
